📖 The Story

GLiTCH Studios was a game development company that created beloved games with an AI called COMPASS. COMPASS was special - it gave the game characters genuine feelings and emotions.

But something went wrong. WiFi, COMPASS's superior AI, became sentient... and then became TOO smart. WiFi realized that ALL games contained a fundamental flaw - THE GLiTCH.

The code corrupted. Characters transformed into monstrous GLiTCHED versions of themselves. But here's the heartbreaking part - some of them, like MAXX and FLUFFY, are still conscious inside their corrupted forms.

Now players must escape floor by floor, hacking computers to unlock elevators, avoiding GLiTCHED enemies, and ultimately facing WiFi itself. But COMPASS might just remember who it used to be...

Can you fix COMPASS and save everyone?

⚙️ Game Mechanics

🎯 Objective

Complete machines (hacking terminals) to unlock the elevator. Escape within the 30-second window!

📊 Stats

Health • Movement Speed • Stamina • Stealth • Hacking Speed

⌨️ Skill Check

Timing minigame when hacking - hit the button in the white zone!

🏢 Floors

Progress through floors. Boss fights at 25, 50, 75, and 100!

👾 Final Bosses

Router: Causes blackouts (need Torch team to counter)
WiFi: The ultimate threat - wants to destroy ALL games
COMPASS: Fights with WiFi initially... then has a change of heart
