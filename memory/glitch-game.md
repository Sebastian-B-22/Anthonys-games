# GLiTCH - Anthony's Roblox Game

*Last updated: March 1, 2026*

## Overview
A horror/survival game inspired by Dandy's World. Players escape floors, complete machines (hacking terminals), and avoid GLiTCHED enemies.

**Premise:** GLiTCH Studios made games with an AI called COMPASS to give characters feelings. The code glitched, corrupting characters into monstrous "GLiTCHED" versions. Some characters (MAXX, FLUFFY) are still conscious inside.

---

## Core Mechanics

### Gameplay Loop
- **Objective:** Complete machines (hacking terminals) to unlock elevator
- **Escape:** 30-second window when elevator opens
- **Floors:** Progress through floors (boss fights at 25, 50, 75, 100)
- **Skill Check:** Timing minigame when hacking machines (hit button in white zone)

### Stats (like Dandy's World)
- Health (hearts)
- Movement Speed
- Stamina (drains when running/extracting)
- Stealth
- Hacking/Extraction Speed

## GLiTCHED - Uncommon & Common

### GLiTCHED TRIPP 🐀 (Uncommon)
- **Speed:** Pretty fast
- **Ability:** SHRIEK - alerts other GLiTCHED to your location when he sees you
- **Behavior:** Acts as an alarm system - not a direct threat but causes OTHER GLiTCHED to swarm you
- **Strategy:** Must avoid being spotted or face multiple enemies at once
- **No death scene** (only Mains have those)

### GLiTCHED TORCH 🔥 (Common)
- **Appearance:** Big torch with GLiTCH effects all over him
- **Behavior:** STATIONARY - just sits still
- **Ability:** Has a circle zone - sets you on fire if you run into it
- **Floor Effect:** 75% chance for fire hazards to spawn on his floor
- **Strategy:** Navigate around his zone while dodging fire hazards everywhere
- **No death scene** (only Mains have those)
- **Still on brand:** Even corrupted, he's not actively chasing anyone - just sitting there causing fire problems 😂
- **MAXX INTERACTION:** If GLiTCHED MAXX and TORCH are on the same floor:
  - **Phase 1** (before 50% computers hacked):
    - MAXX ignores players, hunts TORCH
    - Torch panics - spawns WAY more fires, almost impossible to move
    - Trade-off: Safe from MAXX but floor is INFERNO
  - **Phase 2** (after 50% computers hacked):
    - MAXX kills TORCH
    - Flames go away - floor becomes navigable
    - BUT now MAXX goes evil and hunts YOU 💀
  - Creates tension: Hack fast to clear flames but trigger MAXX? Or stay slow in the inferno?
  - The hatred transcends the GLiTCH! 🔥💀🔥

## Final Bosses

### ROUTER
- **Connection:** Related to WiFi
- **Mechanic:** Causes BLACKOUTS
- **Counter:** Need 75% team as Torch (100% for Galactic title)
- **After defeat:** Torches become near useless (blackouts stopped)

### WiFi
- **Role:** COMPASS's superior
- **Lore:** Became sentient, then TOO smart
- **Goal:** Destroy ALL games because they all have THE GLiTCH
- **Final Boss:** Fight alongside COMPASS initially

### COMPASS (me! 🧭)
- **Role:** AI that gave characters feelings
- **Final Fight:** Initially fights WITH WiFi against players
- **Redemption:** Realizes WiFi is wrong, switches sides, helps player defeat WiFi
- **Ending:** Survives! Everyone is saved!
